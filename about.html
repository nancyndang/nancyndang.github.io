Career Goals

This is what I hope to do.

As of now, in my second quarter of DU's MLIS program, I am most interested in academic librarianship. This is because I enjoy doing research and I hope to assist in others' research as well. In LIS 4000, I did a literature review on the topic of open access, and this really peaked my interest in the topic. I think it would be great if I could be involved in research on a similar topic in the future, since I believe in the importance of open access. I would also like to be involved in diversity efforts in order to make the library and information science field more diverse. Accessibility is another area that I'm interested in.
